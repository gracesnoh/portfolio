Fiat Lux

Poster

Reimagining Berkeley


For Adobe's Design Contest "Berkeley Reimagined" hosted by the Student Technology Council, I illustrated my favorite part of UC Berkeley's campus, Strawberry Creek, and sported our school's motto: "Fiat Lux" (Let there be light). I chose this specific place because I find it very peaceful; it's a place where I can clear my mind and find inspiration for ideas (hence, the light bulb). When I first set eyes on Strawberry Creek, I immediately snapped a picture on my iPhone.

As one of the competition finalists, my design went up on a bus poster that sits at a bus stop right on campus.
