Legacy

Logo, Print, Marketing

First Impressions are Everything


As Marketing Chair of Berkeley ABA, one of the largest business organizations on UC Berkeley's campus, I led our recruitment marketing initiatives to kick off the Spring 2015 school semester. To celebrate our 40th anniversary, we promoted our organization's "Legacy."

I designed a logo that was used in all of our marketing merchandise: flyers, posters, social media, pictures, etc. I chose gold to make it look grand, symbolizing the impressive history of our organization.

When people picked up our flyers or looked at our recruitment event on Facebook for the first time, I wanted to get across the message that ABA is a legitimate organization and fosters successful people.
