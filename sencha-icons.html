Simple is Hard


Throughout my internship at Sencha, I created graphic icons for various parts of the company website and newsletter. For each set of icons, I started by sketching out multiple versions on paper.



The icons needed to be simple, yet completely representative of what features they were being created for. From this experience, I learned that simple is hard. Choosing the single, most correct object or idea as your icon is trickier than I thought it would be!

After much trial and error and checking in with my manager, I came up with some icons that can be viewed here and here.


Takeaways

1. Simple is hard.
2. Prototype first, build later.
